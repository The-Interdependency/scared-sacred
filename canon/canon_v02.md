## 3. Receipts-Live — the Journalism card spec

**Requirement (Erin, canonical):** Journalism cards must bear *recent news posts*; magnitude per tangible metric rendered as *fully instantiated icons appropriate to the particulars*.

**Canonical name for the icon discipline: Isotype.** (Otto Neurath's International System of Typographic Picture Education, 1920s Vienna — the historically correct lineage for exactly this requirement.) Rules, frozen from Neurath:

1. **One icon = one fixed quantity.** Never scale an icon larger to mean "more." More is shown by *more icons*. A card reporting 40,000 displaced people at scale 1 icon = 1,000 prints forty person-icons. The reader counts; the reader is not told.
2. **Scale printed on every card.** `⛺ = 1,000 people` in the card footer. No unlabeled magnitudes.
3. **Icons appropriate to the particulars.** Displacement → tents/figures walking; deaths → the figure lying down (Neurath's own convention); dollars committed → coin stacks; sponsors signed up → raised hands; children → smaller figures, never abstracted into adult icons.
4. **No inference icons.** Icons instantiate *counted things only* — bodies, dollars, signatures, arrivals. Never "hate," never "extremism level." The Engine measures; Isotype displays; neither editorializes. [EDCM AP-6 discipline: no over-precision theater — if the count is an estimate, the final icon prints half-toned.]

**Card mechanics:** Receipts-Live cards are a *slot*, not fixed content — the deck ships with a template and a dated starter set; the living version pulls current documented events (print-and-play PDF regenerated monthly; a0 phase-2 target). R value scales with icon count crossing table-visible thresholds. Reveals the next SE before it resolves, as Journalism did in v0.1.

**Starter card (dated 2026-07, sourced, real):**
> **RECEIPTS-LIVE №1 — "The Raised Hands"**
> 160,000+ Americans applied to privately sponsor refugee families (2023–Jan 2025). ✋ = 2,000 applicants → *eighty hand-icons*.
> $210,000,000+ in private support committed. 💰 = $5M → *forty-two coin-icons*.
> Channel status: suspended by executive order, Jan 20 2025. FY2026 federal cap: 7,500 admissions — printed as *seven and one-half tent-icons* beside the eighty hands.
> **R3.** The dissonance between the hand-icons and the tent-icons is the card's argument. No caption needed.

## 5. The Witness Rule (WA cards — trauma accounts)

**Canonical statement:**

1. **Admission:** Only voluntary, first-person accounts, given by their holder into the light of human attention, enter the WA deck. The holder may withdraw the account at any time, forever, no questions.
2. **The world stops.** When a WA card plays, the Engine freezes: no cards may be played, no auto-turn occurs, no strain accumulates, E(t) is held. Nothing in the game universe outranks the account.
3. **Playthrough:** The account plays all the way through. **Only the account's giver holds the pause.** No other player, no device setting, no timer may interrupt it. (This inverts the original injury: the survivor experienced it without breaks, timeouts, validation, or protection — now the survivor is the only one who can call a break, and the whole world waits.)
4. **Leaving is free, unmarked, and unremarked.** Any player may leave the table during a WA card, for any reason or none, and nothing records it — no track, no marker, no table comment, ever. Coerced listening is forbidden (TIW Article Eight); so is any mechanic that costs the departure. *Ratified constraint (Erin, 2026-07-15): a departure-marking mechanic selects for the most wounded player at the table and is structurally abusable; struck before first printing.* A player who steps out may rejoin at any time, including mid-account, without acknowledgment. The account is owed total attention from those present; it is owed nothing from anyone's endurance.
5. **No censorship of content.** The account's words are the account's words. The only modification permitted is the holder's own.
6. **Closure:** The table reads the Litany. Then, and only then, the Engine resumes — with γ doubled for one round (witnessed truth cools strain faster than any argument in the deck).

**Design ruling [defended]:** The device-lock version of playthrough is rejected — not softened, rejected — because it reproduces the coercion structure the account survived, fails Article Eight, and hands the F-deck its favorite frame ("they force it on you"). The Witness Rule achieves the stated intent — *the account completes, uninterrupted, at the center of total attention* — by making the world stop instead of locking the door.

## 6. Public-figure analog cards — the generation pipeline

**Requirement (Erin, canonical):** Satirical analogs for public figures; card critiques based on observed behavior and transcripts; **no inferences or conjectures of internal states; just the EDCM mechanics.**

**Canonical ruling:** Named-figure cards are **generated, not authored**. The pipeline is the author of record; every card is regenerable and falsifiable from its source transcript. This is stronger satire (documented), stronger law (fair comment on public record), and pure EDCM (the instrument reads; nobody guesses at souls).

**Pipeline (a0 phase-2 target):**

```
transcript (public, dated, sourced)
  → edcmbone segmentation (bones emitted post-morphology)
  → Operator vector O = (P,K,Q,T,S)          [structural]
  → Behavioral vector B = (C,R,D,N,L,O,F,E,I) [structural]
  → card stats: S value = f(strain-side B components)
  → card text: verbatim quoted behavior + metric readout, nothing else
  → satirical analog name (tactic-bearer archetype match from v0.1 deck)
  → art: commissioned caricature in progenitor style (see §7)
```

**Card face contract:** (a) the archetype name the figure's *documented tactics* map to; (b) 1–2 verbatim, dated, sourced quotes; (c) the O/B readout as Isotype icons; (d) S value. **Forbidden on the face:** motive words, diagnosis words, essence words, internal states of any kind. If the transcript shows the Prooftexter circuit, the card says so and shows the numbers; whether the figure *believes* anything is outside the instrument's domain and stays there. [METAPAT domain restraint; EDCM AP-1/AP-2/AP-3/AP-5]

**Falsifiability clause (printed on every analog card back):** *"Dispute this card: run the transcript yourself. The pipeline is open source."*

**Art ruling:** Real photographs are **not** modified. Each analog gets commissioned caricature rendered in a named progenitor style (CAH stark monochrome / Exploding Kittens grotesque / MTG frame parody / Munchkin cartoon), which is the legally and canonically stronger ground for print.
